A few things worth mentioning from my life in 2019.

### Nexus / Snowstorm
The Nexus trusted traveler progrram is awesome -- easy access to Canada, TSA pre-check, etc -- but we had one hell of a time getting them. Approval delays, Trump's government shutdowns twice-postponed our interviews, and finally **snowmageddon** really put into question if we'd make our interview. 

Our drive home from the inverview in Blaine WA was a long, **four** hour, white-knuckled drive in snow. Ugh.

### Texas - San Antonio and Dallas
In the Spring our trip piggybacked on a conference in San Antonio; we hopped over Dallas to be tourists and see my Grandma. We had a great time explosing Dallas.

I don't have any pictures to post because I'm an idiot; I never copied the pictures off my phone before it failed.

### Hiking, Backpacking, and Fishing
This year we've gone hiking and backpacking more times than all other years combined; we even tried the Trailhead Direct service a number of times!  Margaret's Way, Mt. Si, Mailbox Peak, Malakwa Lake, Clear Lake, Ollalie Lake, Lake 22, etc.

This year we also decided to upgrade my ancient REI Quarter Dome tent to a Nemo Dagger 3. Lighter weight makes me happy, and more space makes Miranda happy.

  ![Nemo Dagger 3 at Clear Lake](/img/2019-summary-1.jpg)

One thing that I've really enjoyed doing on our hikes is fishing. I haven't caught anything of note, but just being out there casting is fun. It's also been a nice excuse to explore new places around the region.

  ![Fishing at Lake 22](/img/2019-summary-2.jpg)

### Colorado - Denver and The Great American Beer Festival
In the fall we headed to Denver, CO; a first for both of us. Denver seems like a nice PNW alternative. It has many of the same outdoor activites with less rain (but no ocean). 

  ![GABF](/img/2019-summary-4.jpg)

While there, we attended The Great American Beer Festival with our friends from New York! For years we've talked about attending GABF and this year we finally did it! It was a lot of fun (though a bit of _beer overload_); I would totally go again!

  ![Denver](/img/2019-summary-3.jpg)
