### Seattle to Portland 
In July, I attempted to ride my bicycle 204 miles from [Seattle to Portland](https://ridewithgps.com/ambassador_routes/1217-seattle-to-portland?lang=en) in one day.

I started off strong, but unfortunately around mile 90, I started to get cramps in my right leg. By mile 114, I couldn't pedal anymore. My parents -- who live nearby -- came to pick me up.

I **did not** complete the ride this year. Sad.

![STP](/img/2024-summary-3.jpg)

The thing that frustrates me the most is that I completed training rides as longer than 90 miles without cramps. My bike was the same; my gear was the same; my eating and hydration timing the same; everything was the same! What changed? The weather? Nerves? Donating blood a few weeks before? _sigh_. I'll never know.

The thing I'm most disappointed about is that I didn't restart the ride the second day. I was grumpy and went home. I should have stayed overnight at my parent's house restarted the ride from the 114th mile the next morning. It wouldn't have been completed STP in one day, but at least I would have completed the ride.
