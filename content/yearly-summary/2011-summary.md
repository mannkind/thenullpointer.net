A few things worth mentioning from my life in 2011.

### New Relationship 
Possibly the best thing to happen to me all year happened on April 17th. That's the day that I met Miranda at the 72nd Street Cafe near Green Lake.  As soon as we started talking, I could tell that there was chemistry between us. After that day, I couldn't (and still can't) get enough of her. Honestly, I can say that I've never been happier. Ever. Life is just better with Miranda in it. I'm very thankful and incredibly lucky to have such a intelligent, caring, funny, witty, beautiful, and all-around wonderful woman in my life.
